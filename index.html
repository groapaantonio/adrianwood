About Adrian

Adrian Groapa is a passionate woodcarver and artist known for capturing the spirit of nature and humanity in wood. His work spans detailed depictions of flowers, expressive wildlife, and evocative human figures. With decades of experience and a deep connection to traditional craftsmanship, Adrian brings a rustic warmth to every piece. Each piece is hand carved and painted, showcasing his expertise in the arts.
